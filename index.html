Robot Survive

A 3D WebGL game where you control a robot exploring a procedurally generated city, avoiding collisions with unpredictable cars. The objective is to survive as long as possible while your health (HP) decreases upon each collision.

Controls

  • Move: W, A, S, D
  • Jump: Spacebar
  • Sprint: Shift (hold)
  • Look: Mouse (click on the canvas to activate pointer lock)
